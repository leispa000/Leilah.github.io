About Me

I am a junior pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology and History, originally from Seattle, Washington. As a member of the Humanities and Social Science Scholars Program (H3SP), I engage deeply with interdisciplinary approaches that examine the social forces shaping our world.My academic interests center on race and gender, with a particular focus on how historical systems of inequality continue to influence contemporary social institutions. I am especially interested in the intersections of identity, power, and structural inequality, and how these dynamics manifest in areas such as education, sports, and access to opportunity .
